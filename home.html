For years, women have been oppressed and silenced, unable to use their voices to speak their truth.

Though women have gained many basic human rights over multiple centuries, stories from women and girls all across the world are still kept hidden.

How can we say that there is equality in the world when many women still face the threat of physical violence every single day?

"She Means No" aims to educate and spread awareness on the topic of assault particularly towards women and girls.

Our mission is to disseminate facts and empower victims of assault who are too afraid to come out with their testimonies.

When she says no, she means NO!

She Means NO is not a government organization
